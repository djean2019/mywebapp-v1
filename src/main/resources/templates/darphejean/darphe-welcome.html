Who is Darphe?

Born in Saint-Louis du Nord, in the Northwestern department of Haiti, Darphé received his primary education at the Jean Jacques Dessalines National School in his hometown. He was introduced to music from a very young age. After his 7th grade at Lycée Jean Marie de Lamennais in Saint-Louis du Nord, he went to Port-au-Prince to continue his classical studies at Lycée Pierre Eustache Daniel Fignolé. After completing his classical studies, he was forced to return to Saint-Louis du Nord following the socio-political unrest in the country. He took advantage of this time to write a book titled "Plural Distraction" which will have pleased the majority of young Nordlouisiens.

His University Studies

In 2006, he returned to Port-au-Prince again to begin his university studies at the Institut Universitaire Quisqueya-Amérique (INUQUA), computer science option. Being passionate about mathematics, a year later he enrolled at the State University of Haiti where he passed the entrance examination to the École Normale Supérieure. He had to redouble his efforts to be able to maintain university studies in two different faculties because almost all his days were dedicated to his studies.

In 2019, he went to the USA for a Masters in Computer Science at Maharishi International University.

Hobbies

His favorite hobbies are of course: programming more precisely java, music, dancing, and sometimes cinema.
